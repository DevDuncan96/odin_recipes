Potato Soup

Potato soup

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Potatoes
  • Bacon
  • Chicken Stock

Steps

  1. Chop veggies
  2. Chop bacon into 1/2 inch pieces
  3. Sauté bacon
  4. Remove bacon leaving bacon grease in pan
  5. Add veggies to pad, sauté until tender
  6. Add 1 cup chicken stock and 1 cup water to pan
  7. Add potatoes
  8. Bring to boil and reduce to simmer, let cook 45 minutes
  9. Use emersion blender and blend until soup is smooth
